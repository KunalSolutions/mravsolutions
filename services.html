Annual Maintenance Contract (AMC)

At MR AV Solutions & Services, we offer comprehensive AMC services for AV and IT systems.

Audio System Maintenance

  • Inspection of PA systems, microphones, speakers, and amplifiers
  • Fine-tuning and calibration for optimal sound quality
  • Replacement of faulty cables, connectors, and components

Video & Display Maintenance

  • Routine checks for LED walls, projectors, and digital signage
  • Calibration of color, brightness, and resolution settings
  • Cleaning and servicing of lenses, screens, and display panels

Video Conferencing

  • Testing of conferencing software (Zoom, Teams, Webex)
  • Camera and microphone diagnostics
  • Network troubleshooting for smooth connectivity

Live Streaming & Broadcast

  • Inspection of streaming hardware and software
  • Preventive maintenance of encoders, servers, and networks
  • Performance optimization for high-quality live streams
